Fav Cartoon Characters!!

Snoopy Snoopy is one of the most iconic characters from the Peanuts comic strip. He is Charlie Brown's pet beagle and is known for his vivid imagination, where he often pretends to be a World War I flying ace battling the Red Baron.
SpongeBob SquarePants SpongeBob SquarePants is the cheerful and energetic main character. He is a sea sponge who lives in a pineapple house under the sea in Bikini Bottom. SpongeBob works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and is known for his infectious optimism, enthusiastic personality, and love for his job.
Bart Simpson Bart Simpson is one of the main characters from The Simpsons. Bart is known for his rebellious, mischievous, and prank-loving personality. His signature catchphrases include “Eat my shorts!” and “Don’t have a cow, man!”
Clarence Clarence is a 9-year-old boy known for his wide-eyed optimism, childlike wonder, and friendly personality. Clarence is always enthusiastic and finds joy in the simplest things, whether it’s playing outside, making new friends, or exploring his small suburban world.
Uncle Grandpa Uncle Grandpa who is described as the "magical uncle and grandpa to everyone in the world." Uncle Grandpa is an eccentric, fun-loving character with a big belly, a red fanny pack, and a quirky sense of humor. He travels around in his magical RV, helping children and others with their problems in the most bizarre and unexpected ways.
